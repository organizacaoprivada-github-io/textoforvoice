Frequently Asked Questions

How to get started?
You can use our free plan with 100 converts per week by registering, For full access to our features and voices buy our paid basic, premium and business plans respectively.
Will my subscription auto-renew?
No. At this moment you need to re-activate your subscription every month manually.
How can I cancel my subscription?
At this moment, we do not auto-renew any plans and your plan will expire at the end of the period, so we did not add a cancel button.
Do you count characters based on Converts or Downloads?
Text characters are counted on Converts, not on downloads. Every time you click "Convert to Speech", we count the text characters.
How many hours of TTS voiceovers, I can create with 500,000 text characters.
500,000 text characters are equivalent to 12 to 13 hours of text to speech audio generation.
I want to upgrade my plan from Basic to Premium or Business plan?
For usage under 10,000 text characters and a change to premium or business plan within 48 hours, we automatically calculate and deduct the amount of your last plan (Basic plan) and give you that discount on your new plan (Premium or Business).
Do you really offer unlimited converts?
No, Offering a truly unlimited converts is impossible from a technological point of view. Convert text into realistic human-like speech takes a tremendous amount of CPU and GPU power to run AI models and output voices. So we have a limit of characters per month. For businesses and large creators we offer a custom enterprise plan.
What to do If my subscription is over?
Visit the "Subscription" page in your profile menu to re-activate your subscription. You have to buy plans again, just like the first time.
Can I get a refund?
If you are not satisfied with our platform, you can contact us within 5 days and usage under 10,000 text characters since your first payment. We will process it within one business day. No hard feelings, no questions asked.
Who owns the copyright for text-to-speech audio ?
By subscribing to our Paid Plans, you own the full copyright of any voice speech created with VoiceMaker, forever.
Can I use Voicemaker's text to speech voices for my YouTube videos?
Yes! You can use our voice audios for your YouTube videos.
What languages do VoiceMaker support?
Currently we supports 130+ languages worldwide, such as English (US, UK, AU, IN, Welsh), Spanish (Castilian, Mexican, US), German, Dutch, Danish, French, India (Hindi, Gujarati, Bengali, Kannada, Malayalam, Tamil, Telgu), Italian, Icelandic, Japanese, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Turkish, Welsh, Vietnamese, Korean, Norwegian, Portuguese, Brazilian, Romanian, Indonesian, Arabic, Mandarin Chinese & lots more. Please check our AI voices page for the details.
What types of payment do you accept?
We accept all major cards such as VISA, Mastercard. Payments secured and powered by Stripe.com, PayPal & RazorPay (UPI, GPay, PhonePe & Bank Transfer) for Indian users.
Do you offer custom plans?
Yes, we can make a custom enterprise plan for you, please email to [email protected]

Feel free to contact us if you don't find the answer to your question in our FAQs.
